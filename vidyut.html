From Village Dreams to National Success

Growing up in a small village in Odisha, I witnessed firsthand the stark divide in educational opportunities between urban and rural India. While city students had access to world-class coaching institutes, unlimited study materials, and expert guidance, my friends in the village struggled with basic resources, outdated textbooks, and no proper mentorship.

I was fortunate enough to receive quality education and secured Rank 1 in JEE Advanced. But this success came with a responsibility ‚Äì a responsibility to bridge this gap that prevents millions of talented students from realizing their potential.
